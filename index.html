This is Hyla, an artist + farmhand + friendly hermit. Root Cellar Studios is a space I've created for general creative mischief and seasonal snacking sharing. I love ol' fashion ink sketching, paint occasional murals and am a novice (but completely enchanted) ceramics student. You can follow my most recent shinanigans on instagram at @rootcellarstudios, or send me an email at info@rootcellarstudios.org .

Thanks for visiting friends!
